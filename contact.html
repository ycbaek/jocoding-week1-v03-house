Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for homes on HomeFind?

Visit our home page and use the search filters to narrow results by location, price range, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, property type, and more. You can also save your favorite listings for easy comparison later.

Is HomeFind free to use?

Yes. Searching listings, saving favorites, and contacting realtors through our platform is completely free for home buyers and renters.

What areas does HomeFind cover?

We currently have listings in Seattle, Redmond, Bellevue, and Kirkland in Washington state, as well as Somerville, Massachusetts. We are actively working to expand to additional markets.

How does the AI property classification work?

When you click on a listing, our machine learning model analyzes the property image and provides a classification estimate (house, condo, or multifamily). This is powered by a Teachable Machine model and serves as an additional data point for your evaluation.

Can I list my property on HomeFind?

If you are interested in listing your property, please contact us using the form above and select "I want to sell my home" as the subject. A member of our team will discuss the process with you.

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, please call our office during business hours.
