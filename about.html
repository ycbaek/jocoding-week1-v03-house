About HomeFind

We believe finding a home should be straightforward, transparent, and even enjoyable. Here is who we are and why we built this platform.

Our Mission

HomeFind was created with a simple goal: make real estate search accessible to everyone. Whether you are a first-time buyer nervous about your biggest financial decision, a renter looking for the right neighborhood, or a homeowner exploring what your property is worth, we want to give you the tools and information you need to make confident choices.

We combine a powerful property search engine with AI-driven insights, local market data, and direct connections to experienced realtors. The result is a platform that respects your time and puts your needs first.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Listings

Browse hundreds of homes for sale and rent across the Pacific Northwest and the Greater Boston area. Filter by price, size, type, and more.

AI Property Classification

Our machine learning model analyzes listing images to help verify property types, adding an extra layer of transparency to your search.

Market Intelligence

Real-time price averages, median calculations, and neighborhood-level trend analysis to keep you informed about where the market is heading.

Expert Connections

Reach out to local realtors directly through our platform. Get personalized advice, schedule tours, and have your questions answered by professionals.

Service Areas

We currently serve the following markets, with plans to expand:

  • Seattle, WA — From Capitol Hill and Ballard to Beacon Hill and West Seattle, we cover all of Seattle's diverse neighborhoods.
  • Redmond, WA — A family-friendly tech hub with excellent schools, trails, and easy access to the Eastside corridor.
  • Bellevue, WA — A booming urban center with luxury condos, modern developments, and established residential neighborhoods.
  • Kirkland, WA — A charming waterfront city offering a blend of small-town feel and metropolitan convenience.
  • Somerville, MA — A vibrant, transit-connected community near Boston with a thriving arts and food scene.

Our Values

Transparency First

We show you real data. Our price averages, median figures, and AI classifications are all visible so you can evaluate properties with full context. We never hide information or steer you toward a particular listing.

User-Centered Design

Every feature we build starts with a question: does this genuinely help someone find a better home? We keep our interface clean, our filters powerful, and our content honest. No pop-ups, no gimmicks — just useful tools.

Community Focus

Real estate is local. We invest in understanding the neighborhoods we serve — the schools, parks, transit options, dining scenes, and community character that make each area unique. Our neighborhood guides and market reports reflect that commitment.

Continuous Improvement

We are always refining our search algorithms, expanding our coverage, and adding new features based on user feedback. HomeFind is a platform that grows with you.

Get in Touch

Have questions, feedback, or ideas for how we can improve? We would love to hear from you. Visit our Contact page to send us a message, or explore our Buying Guide and Market Trends for more helpful resources.
